## How Agents Increase Revenue

### 1. Higher Email Open Rates
**Current: 24% | Target: 30% (+25% more opens)**
- Agent A/B tests subject lines daily
- Winning variant allocated 70% of sends
- Result: +25 more opens/day = +6 more replies/day = +1.5 more closes/month


### 2. Better Prospect Targeting
**Current: TBD* close rate | Target: (+60% improvement with the AI agent)**
- Agent refines segmentation based on weekly performance
- Focuses budget on highest-converting segments
- Removes low-engagement prospects early


### 3. Faster Sales Cycles
**Current: TBD* days to close | Target: 14 days (-33%)**
- Agent triggers LinkedIn follow-up immediately on email click
- Social media engagement pre-warms prospects
- Closer gets warm handoff with 70%+ engagement score

**Revenue impact: +3 extra closes/month per product**

### 4. Geographic Expansion
**Current: Netherlands only | Target: Worldwide (Year 2)**
- Agent supports multi-language outreach
- Prospect database expands 3x
- Same close rate across regions

**Revenue impact: 3x revenue multiplier**

### 5. Product Expansion
**Current: 5 products | Target: 7-8 products (Year 2027)**
- Agent architecture scales to new products
- Existing playbooks reused
- New customer segments tapped

**Revenue impact: +40-60% additional revenue**
