**KvanBroekhoven and the company Bayata measure success on 4 dimensions: Acquisition, Quality, Efficiency, Growth.**

Both parties must hit targets for partnership to continue to next phase.

## Monthly Reporting

**Both parties receive every month:**

1. **Acquisition dashboard** — Prospects, contacts, deals, closes by product
2. **Quality dashboard** — Open rates, reply rates, close rates, retention
3. **Financial dashboard** — Revenue, commission, costs, profitability
4. **Health scores** — Agent performance, product health, customer quality
5. **Pipeline forecast** — Next 30/60/90 days projections
6. **A/B test results** — Winning variants, lift metrics, recommendations
7. **Risk alerts** — Any metric trending below success threshold
