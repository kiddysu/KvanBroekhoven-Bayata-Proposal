## The Problem

- Mestloket market is huge (16,800 Dutch farmers)
- But farmers are fragmented (large, medium, small)
- Regulatory deadline (2026) creates urgency
- Manual prospecting doesn't scale
- No data-driven prioritization of best leads
- Social media presence could be stronger

---

## The Solution: Mestloket Agent

**Intelligent Agent That:**

1. **Discovers** qualified farmers via Lusha (10-50/day)
2. **Enriches** data by calling your APIs
3. **Scores** farmers 0-100 (your scoring rules)
4. **Routes** intelligently:
   - Score 80+: Warm outreach (email + LinkedIn)
   - Score 50-80: Medium outreach (email first)
   - Score <50: Nurture queue (weekly content)
5. **Personalizes** messages by farm size & urgency
6. **Sends** via email + LinkedIn (multi-channel)
7. **Nurtures** with follow-ups (5-day sequences)
8. **Learns** via A/B testing (optimize weekly)
